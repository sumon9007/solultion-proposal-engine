# Rule: Proposal Structure Rules

Every proposal produced by this workspace MUST follow these structural rules. Deviations require explicit user approval.

---

## Required Sections (in order)

All proposals must contain these 13 sections as top-level headings:

| # | Section | Heading Variants Accepted |
|---|---------|--------------------------|
| 1 | Cover Page | "Cover", title block |
| 2 | Executive Summary | "Executive Summary" |
| 3 | Understanding of Requirements | "Understanding of Requirements", "Client Requirements", "Your Requirements" |
| 4 | Proposed Solution | "Proposed Solution", "Our Solution", "Solution Overview" |
| 5 | Scope of Work | "Scope of Work", "Scope", "Statement of Work" |
| 6 | Deliverables | "Deliverables", "Key Deliverables" |
| 7 | Assumptions & Dependencies | "Assumptions", "Assumptions and Dependencies", "Assumptions & Dependencies" |
| 8 | Exclusions | "Exclusions", "Out of Scope" |
| 9 | Indicative Pricing | "Pricing", "Investment", "Indicative Pricing", "Commercial Summary" |
| 10 | Timeline | "Timeline", "Project Timeline", "Delivery Plan", "High-Level Plan" |
| 11 | About Us | "About Us", "Company Profile", "Why Us", "Our Credentials" |
| 12 | Next Steps | "Next Steps", "How to Proceed" |
| 13 | Legal Notes | "Legal Notes", "Legal", "Terms and Conditions", "Disclaimer" |

---

## Section Content Rules

### Executive Summary
- Must mention the client name at least once
- Must state the proposed solution in the first 2 paragraphs
- Minimum 100 words, maximum 200 words

### Proposed Solution
- Must reference at least one specific technology, platform, or methodology
- Must connect to at least one stated client outcome

### Scope of Work
- Must include a change control statement
- Must include a table or list distinguishing in-scope from out-of-scope items
- Must specify client responsibilities

### Deliverables
- Must be a table or numbered list
- Must include at minimum: what the deliverable is, its format, and when it is delivered
- Minimum 3 deliverables

### Assumptions & Dependencies
- Must include at least 3 assumptions
- Each assumption must follow the format: "It is assumed that [condition]..."

### Exclusions
- Must be an explicit bulleted or numbered list
- Minimum 3 exclusions

### Indicative Pricing
- Must include a "Total Investment" or equivalent summary line
- Must state VAT status (inclusive / exclusive)
- Must state payment terms

### Timeline
- Must include duration or date references (not just phase names)

### Legal Notes
- Must include: validity period, confidentiality statement, liability limitation

---

## Ordering Rules

- Sections must appear in the order above
- Sub-sections may be added within sections freely
- New top-level sections may be added between existing ones with user approval, but none of the 13 may be removed

---

## Cover Page Requirements

The cover page must include:
- Proposal title
- Client name
- Prepared by (company name)
- Document reference (PROP-YYYY-MM-DD-CLIENTSLUG)
- Date
- Version number
- Validity statement (e.g. "Valid for 30 days from date of issue")
