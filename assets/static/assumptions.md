## Assumptions & Dependencies

The following assumptions have been made in preparing this proposal. If any assumption proves to be incorrect, the scope, timeline, or cost of this engagement may be affected. Any such change will be managed through our formal Change Request process.

### General Assumptions

1. It is assumed that the client will appoint a named Project Sponsor and a named Technical Lead for the duration of the engagement, with sufficient authority to make decisions within agreed timeframes.

2. It is assumed that the client will make relevant stakeholders available for workshops, interviews, and reviews as required by the project plan. Unavailability of key stakeholders may cause delays to the timeline.

3. It is assumed that the client has, or will obtain, all necessary licences, subscriptions, and software entitlements required for the proposed solution prior to the commencement of any technical work.

4. It is assumed that the client's existing infrastructure (networks, servers, end-user devices) meets the minimum requirements for the proposed solution. Any remediation required to bring infrastructure to a suitable baseline is not included in this proposal.

5. It is assumed that the client will complete any prerequisite tasks, data cleansing, or environment preparation activities as identified in the Scope of Work before the relevant project phase commences.

6. It is assumed that the client will provide timely responses to information requests, review cycles, and sign-off requests within the timeframes agreed in the project plan. Delays in client response may affect the project timeline and cost.

7. It is assumed that third-party vendors and system owners whose systems are in scope will co-operate with the project team and provide the necessary access, documentation, and support.

8. It is assumed that the working environment is safe and compliant with all relevant health, safety, and security requirements for our engineers to perform on-site work where required.

### Technical Assumptions

9. It is assumed that the client has suitable internet connectivity and bandwidth to support the proposed cloud solution or remote working requirements.

10. It is assumed that the client's domain and DNS are correctly configured and accessible to our team throughout the engagement.

11. It is assumed that administrator-level access to all relevant systems will be provided to the project team before technical work commences, and that access will remain available for the duration of the engagement.
