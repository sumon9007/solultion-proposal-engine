## Our Consultancy Services

[Your Company Name]'s Consultancy practice helps organisations make confident technology decisions. We provide independent assessment, strategic advice, and actionable roadmaps — grounded in technical reality, not vendor marketing.

### What We Do

Our consultancy engagements typically follow a structured approach:

**Discovery & Assessment**
We work with your leadership and technical teams to understand your current state — your environment, your processes, your constraints, and your ambitions. We don't just look at technology; we look at the business context that technology must serve.

**Analysis & Benchmarking**
We assess your environment against industry standards, best-practice frameworks (such as the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework, NIST, or ISO 27001), and peer organisations. We identify gaps, risks, and opportunities with evidence, not opinion.

**Recommendations & Roadmap**
We produce a prioritised technology roadmap that is realistic, costed, and aligned to your business goals. We distinguish between what you must do, what you should do, and what you could do — and we give you the information to make those decisions confidently.

**Business Case Development**
Where required, we help you build the business case for investment — quantifying risks, modelling costs and savings, and presenting options to your board or finance committee.

### Our Approach

We are technology agnostic advisors. Our recommendations are based on your needs, not vendor relationships. We will tell you what the right solution is for your organisation — including when the right answer is to stay with what you have.

Every engagement is led by a senior consultant with direct experience in the relevant technology domain. We do not delegate advisory work to junior team members.
