### What We Do

Our implementation practice spans the full project lifecycle:

**Solution Design**
Before we build anything, we design it. Our architects produce detailed solution designs that are reviewed, approved, and baselined before implementation begins. This prevents scope creep and ensures that what we deliver matches what was agreed.

**Build and Configuration**
Our certified engineers configure and deploy technology solutions to your agreed specification. We follow documented standards and maintain build records for everything we deliver.

**Testing and Quality Assurance**
Every deployment is tested before it touches production. We conduct functional testing, integration testing, and user acceptance testing — with sign-off gates at each stage.

**Migration and Go-Live**
We plan migrations with precision: cut-over windows, rollback plans, and real-time support during go-live. We do not leave clients unsupported at the point of maximum risk.

**Knowledge Transfer and Handover**
At project close, we provide full documentation of what has been built and trained your team to operate it. We do not create dependency — we build capability.

### Our Delivery Approach

We use a structured project delivery methodology with defined phases, milestones, and governance:

- **Project Initiation** — Charter, RACI, plan, risk register
- **Design Phase** — Solution architecture, HLD/LLD, design approval
- **Build Phase** — Configuration, scripting, environment preparation
- **Test Phase** — Test plans, execution, defect resolution, UAT sign-off
- **Go-Live** — Migration, cut-over, hypercare support
- **Project Close** — Documentation, knowledge transfer, lessons learned
