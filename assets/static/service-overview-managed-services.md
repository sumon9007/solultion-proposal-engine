- Service desk: Business hours (8am–6pm, Monday–Friday)
- Incident and request management
- Remote support for end users
- Monthly service report

#### Professional
*Proactive management for organisations that cannot afford unexpected downtime.*

- Everything in Essentials, plus:
- 24x7 critical incident response
- Proactive infrastructure monitoring and alerting
- Scheduled patching (OS and third-party applications)
- Monthly infrastructure health review
- Quarterly service review meeting

#### Enterprise
*Strategic partnership for organisations where technology is business-critical.*

- Everything in Professional, plus:
- Dedicated Technical Account Manager

- Change Advisory Board (CAB) participation

### Service Standards

All service tiers include the following service level commitments:

| Priority | Definition | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| P1 — Critical | Complete outage / major impact | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| P2 — High | Significant degradation | 2 hours | 8 hours |
| P3 — Medium | Non-critical issue | 4 business hours | Next business day |
| P4 — Low | Request / general query | Next business day | 5 business days |

*P1/P2 response times apply 24x7 for Professional and Enterprise tiers. Essentials tier: business hours only.*

### Onboarding

All new managed service clients go through a structured onboarding process:

1. **Discovery** — Document your environment, contacts, and escalation paths
2. **Tooling deployment** — Deploy monitoring agents and remote management tools
3. **Knowledge base build** — Capture system details, known issues, and procedures
4. **Hypercare** — 30-day intensive support period with daily check-ins
5. **Business as usual** — Transition to standard service model
