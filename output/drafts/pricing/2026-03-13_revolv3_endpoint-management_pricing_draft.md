## Revolv3 — Endpoint Management & Managed Service

| Phase | Component | Unit | Qty | Rate | Total |
|-------|-----------|------|-----|------|-------|
| Phase 1 | Endpoint Management Implementation | Project | 1 | Complimentary | $0.00 |

---

### Phase 1 — Implementation

The full endpoint management implementation — including Intune configuration, security baseline deployment, application packaging and deployment, device enrolment, privilege management setup, and documentation — is provided **complimentary** as part of this engagement.

**Implementation total: $0.00**

---

### Phase 2 — Ongoing Managed Service

The ongoing managed service covers compliance monitoring, patch management, application update management, tiered support (3 SLA tiers), monthly compliance reporting, and change management — for all managed devices in scope.

| Item | Detail | Monthly Rate | Devices | Monthly Total |
|------|--------|-------------|---------|--------------|
| Managed Endpoint Service | Per device per month | $15.00 | 40 | **$600.00** |

**Monthly managed service total: $600.00**

The managed service fee covers the following activities each month:
- Compliance monitoring and monthly compliance report
- Patch management (Windows and macOS, per defined deferral schedule)
- Application update management and monthly version review
- Tiered support desk (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3 via Jira, shared mailbox, and Teams)
- Change management (written approval process per Revolv3 requirements)
- Real-time non-compliance alerting to Revolv3 IT point of contact

---

### Device Count and Scaling

The managed service fee is calculated per managed device. Should the device count change, the monthly fee adjusts accordingly:

| Device Count | Monthly Fee |
|-------------|------------|
| 40 (current scope) | $600.00 |
| 45 | $675.00 |
| 50 | $750.00 |

Changes to device count are effective from the first day of the following billing month.

---

### Payment Terms

- **Phase 1 (Implementation):** No charge.
- **Phase 2 (Managed Service):** Invoiced monthly in advance from the managed service commencement date.
- Payment due within 30 days of invoice date.

### VAT and Taxes

All prices are exclusive of VAT and any applicable local taxes, which will be applied at the prevailing rate.
